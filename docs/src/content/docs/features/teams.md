---
title: Teams
description: Create and manage teams with persistent membership for signed-in users.
---

Teams allow signed-in users to organize their colleagues into groups with shared access to rooms and settings. This is ideal for organizations that run regular planning sessions.

## Creating an Account

To use Teams, you'll need a Sakiyomi account:

1. Click **Sign Up** in the header
2. Enter your email and password
3. You're ready to create teams!

## Creating a Team

1. Click your avatar in the header to open the menu
2. Select **Teams**
3. Click **Create Team**
4. Enter a team name and URL slug (e.g., `my-company`)
5. Click **Create Team**

You'll be redirected to your new team's dashboard as the owner.

## Team Roles

### Owner
- Full control over the team
- Manage team settings
- Invite and remove members
- Delete the team

### Member
- Access team rooms
- Participate in estimation sessions
- View team information

## Team Members

Navigate to your team's **Members** page to:

- View all team members and their roles
- Invite new members
- Remove members (owners only)

### Inviting Members

1. Go to your team's Members page
2. Click **Invite**
3. Share the invite link with your colleague
4. They'll be added to the team when they accept

## Team Settings

Owners can access team settings to:

- Update the team name
- Change the team URL slug
- Configure default room settings
- Delete the team

## Navigating Teams

Access your teams from anywhere:

1. Click your avatar in the header
2. Select **Teams** from the dropdown
3. Choose a team from your list

## Benefits of Teams

- **Persistent membership** - No need to rejoin rooms each session
- **Shared history** - Team rooms are easily accessible
- **Centralized management** - One place to manage all team members
