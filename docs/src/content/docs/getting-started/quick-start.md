---

Get your team estimating in under a minute with this quick start guide.

## 1. Create a Room

1. Go to [sakiyomi.dev](https://sakiyomi.dev)
2. Click **Create Room**
3. Enter a room name (e.g., "Sprint 42 Planning")
4. Click **Create**

## 2. Share the Link

Copy the room URL and share it with your team via Slack, Teams, or email.

## 3. Join the Room

When team members open the link, they'll:
1. Choose an avatar
2. Enter their name
3. Click **Join Room**

## 4. Start Estimating

The room manager can:
1. Enter a story/ticket topic
2. Click **Start Voting**
3. Wait for everyone to vote
4. Click **Reveal Votes**

## 5. Discuss & Repeat

After revealing:
- Discuss any large differences
- Click **New Round** to estimate the next story

## Tips

- **Permanent rooms**: Toggle "Permanent room" when creating to keep the room active
- **Manager controls**: The room creator is automatically a manager
- **Custom votes**: Managers can customize vote options in room settings
